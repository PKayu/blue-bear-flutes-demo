The Maker

Charlie Mato-Toyela — "Blue Bear"

My name translates to "Blue Bear" in the Lakota tradition, and it's the name I've built this craft around for over 38 years. Every flute that leaves my hands is shaped, tuned, and tested — not assembled from a template, not mass-produced.

I started making flutes because I loved the music. What began as personal craft became a community of thousands of players worldwide — from beginners who had never touched an instrument to professional musicians seeking a voice they couldn't find elsewhere.

Whether you choose an old-style cedar flute or a modern 3D-printed instrument, you're getting decades of acoustic refinement in every note.

Charlie Mato-Toyela
Founder & Master Flute Maker, Blue Bear Flutes

Why Blue Bear

What Sets Our Flutes Apart

38 years of refinement means every design decision has been earned — not guessed at.

🌳

Hand-Selected Wood

Eastern red cedar, western cedar, purpleheart, padauk, yellowheart — each species chosen for its acoustic properties and natural beauty. No two flutes are identical.

🎵

Precision Tuning

Every flute ships tuned and tested. Choose 440Hz standard, 432Hz earth tone, or 528Hz at no extra charge — just include a note with your order.

🎓

Free Lessons Included

Your purchase comes with access to a full library of free video lessons on YouTube and Instagram. Charlie teaches personally — no one knows these instruments like their maker.

🌎

Ships Worldwide

Free shipping throughout the United States. Affordable international rates calculated at checkout. Every flute is carefully packaged and guaranteed to arrive in perfect playing condition.
