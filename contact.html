Quick Answers

Common Questions

How long does shipping take?

Most orders ship within 7–14 business days. Specialty or custom flutes may take a bit longer as they are made to order. U.S. shipping is always free.

Can I get my flute in 432Hz or 528Hz?

Yes — at no extra charge. Just include a note with your order specifying your preferred tuning. All keyed flutes can be tuned to 440Hz, 432Hz, or 528Hz.

What flute is best for a complete beginner?

The Old-Style Eastern Red Cedar 5-Hole Flute or the Modern Series 5-Hole are both excellent starting points. Both are forgiving to learn and rewarding to play. When in doubt, ask Charlie.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes — we ship worldwide at affordable rates. International shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on your location and package size. Every flute is carefully packaged for safe delivery.
